The Journey ofOur Integrated Resorts
Out of 19 global operators, Genting Singapore was awarded the license to develop one of Singapore's Integrated Resorts, a concept initiated by the government to boost tourism with iconic developments comprising a comprehensive range of amenities. Through strategic collaboration with the government, joint infrastructure development, and strong community partnerships, Genting Singapore exceeded expectations. By 2019, the resort had played a pivotal role in increasing Singapore’s international visitor arrivals to 19.1 million and tourism receipts to S$27.8 billion – far surpassing the initial goal of doubling the 8.5 million visitors from 2005.
